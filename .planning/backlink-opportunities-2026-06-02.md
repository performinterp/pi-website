- **BBC has zero indexed PI mentions** despite PI's broadcast work. Either editorial policy on naming suppliers, or the mentions live on un-indexed pages (PDFs, scheduling tools). Worth a direct ask to the BBC Access team.
- **Wikipedia is the biggest strategic gap.** PI clears notability (Wembley appointment, Coronation, Little Mix legal precedent) but has no article. That's the single highest-value long-term asset.
- **Ireland (Croke Park, Electric Picnic, MCD, 3Olympia) returned nothing.** If PI has worked in Ireland, it's not on those venues' own websites.
