Our story

Built from the community up — by someone who understood the need first-hand.

Performance Interpreting grew from responding to Deaf audiences who wanted access — not from selling a service nobody asked for. Founded by Marie, who is hard of hearing herself, PI was built on genuine understanding of what it means to be left out of the live experience.
